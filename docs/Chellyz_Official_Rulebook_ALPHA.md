# Chellyz Official Rule Book (ALPHA)

Rulebook is subject to change.\
Evolve or Dissolve!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Game Objective

KO your opponent's creatures.

The first player to knock out **4 opponent Chellyz** wins the match.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Deck Construction

## Deck Size

50 Cards Total

## Each Deck May Contain

-   Level 1 Chellyz
-   Level 2 Chellyz
-   Level 3 Chellyz
-   ONE Universal Chelly
-   Support Cards

## Evolution Chain Rule

If you include a Level 2 or Level 3 Chelly, you must include its full
evolution chain:

Level 1 → Level 2 → Level 3

Level 2 and Level 3 Chellyz cannot be played directly from hand. They
must enter play through evolution only.

## Universal Chelly

-   Maximum 1 per deck
-   May only be placed onto a fully evolved Level 3 Chelly
-   Cannot be played directly from hand

## Support Card Limits

-   Max 10 total Support Cards per deck
-   Support Cards include Memory Artifacts and Flash Relics
-   Maximum 3 Flash Relics
-   Flash Relics count toward the 10 total

## Main Deck Composition

The 50-card deck contains Chellyz and Memory Artifacts. Energy Bloom
Cards (EB) are drawn separately from the Energy Bloom Deck.

## Recommended Deck Build

-   14--18 Level 1 Chellyz
-   10 Memory Artifacts

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Game Setup

## Deck Preparation

1.  Prepare your 50-card deck.
2.  Search deck and:
    -   Place one Level 1 Chelly face-down in Active Zone.
    -   Place one Level 1 Chelly face-up in Bench Zone.
3.  Shuffle deck thoroughly.

## Energy Bloom Deck

Keep separate and accessible.

## Opening Hand

Draw 6 cards.

## Starting Energy

Draw 1 Energy Bloom Card into your Energy Zone.

## Game Start

Flip Active Chelly simultaneously.

## First Turn

Highest Speed goes first. If tied, flip a coin.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Zones

Active Zone -- 1 battling Chelly\
Bench Zone -- Up to 3 reserve Chellyz\
Prep Zone -- 1 staged Memory Artifact\
Energy Zone -- Up to 7 Energy Bloom Cards\
Hand -- No size limit

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Turn Structure

## Draw Phase

-   Draw 1 Deck Card
-   Draw 1 Energy Bloom Card (if under 7)

## Sacrifice Phase (Optional)

Discard 1 Chelly → Gain 1 Energy Bloom\
- Max 2 sacrifices per turn - Max 7 EB total - Only Chelly cards may be
sacrificed

## Evolution Phase

Evolve 1 Chelly (if applicable).

## Retreat Phase (Optional)

Swap Active with Benched. Ends turn immediately.

## Bench Swap Phase (Optional)

-   Replace 1 Benched Chelly with Level 1 from hand
-   Cost: Discard 1 EB
-   One swap per turn
-   Each Benched Chelly can only be swapped once per game
-   Does NOT end turn

## Bench Fill Phase (Optional)

Place Level 1 Chellyz into open Bench slots.

## Support Prep Phase (Optional)

Play 1 Memory Artifact OR activate Flash Relic.

## Action Phase

Choose one: - Attack (Base ATK) - Special (Discard required EB)

## Piercing Roll Phase (Optional)

Cost: 1 EB\
Roll d6 (once per turn)

1--3: Nothing\
4--5: Opponent DEF --10 (min 0)\
6: Opponent DEF = 0

Applies to current attack only.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Evolution System

Level 1 → Level 2 → Level 3 → Universal

Energy Costs: - L1 → L2 = 1 EB - L2 → L3 = 2 EB - L3 → Universal = 3 EB

Damage carries over. Stats activate next turn if evolved in Active Zone.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Retreat Rules

-   Once per turn
-   Must have Benched Chelly
-   Ends turn immediately
-   Cannot replace with hand

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Knock Out (KO)

A Chelly is KO'd at 0 HP.

If no Benched Chelly available → Penalty State.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Penalty System

## Partial Penalty

If no Benched Chelly:

Your next turn: - Draw 1 Deck + 1 EB - Place Level 1 on Bench - No other
actions

Opponent turn: - Normal turn but no attacks possible

## Full Penalty

If no Level 1 in hand: - Draw 1 Deck Card (no EB) - If none drawn: -
Draw 1 - Discard 1 - Repeat each turn

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Damage Formula

(Base ATK or Special\
+ Strength Bonus +10\
-- Weakness Penalty --10\
-- Opponent DEF)\
= Total Damage (minimum 0)

Piercing modifies DEF before calculation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Support Card System

Memory Artifacts: - Enter Prep Zone - Activate next turn - Lost if
Chelly KO'd before activation - Only 1 staged at a time

Flash Relics: - Activate instantly - Max 3 per deck - Count toward 10
total

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Universal Chelly

-   1 per deck
-   Strong vs Non-Universal
-   Weak vs Universal
-   Must be placed on Level 3
-   Costs 3 EB

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Victory Conditions

-   KO 4 Chellyz to win

Tie Breakers: 1. Most Active HP 2. Most EB 3. Highest Level Chelly 4.
Draw

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Glossary

ATK = Attack\
DEF = Defense\
SP = Special\
EB = Energy Bloom\
KO = HP reaches 0\
AC = Active Chelly\
BC = Benched Chelly
